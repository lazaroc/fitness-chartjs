Monthly Gym Check-ins

Here's a bar chart that shows check-ins to the gym by month. I pulled this data directly from my LA Fitness membership account. It is cool to be able to look back and see how check-ins vary month to month.

For example, I can see check-ins are trending downward so far in 2017 which makes sense. We ended up moving to a new house in March which is a further distance to the gym. We also started a crossfit membership in April.
